# NephroCare – Predictive Healthcare Support System

NephroCare is a healthcare support system designed to assist in early detection and monitoring of kidney-related conditions using machine learning techniques. What if we tell you that 1 in 7 Americans suffer every day from chronic kidney diseases? For hospitals, that means more patients than ever and by extension fewer nurses to care for them. Studies show that just one additional patient added to a nurse’s workload increases the risk of patient death by 7%, while also raising nurse workload by 23%. And yet, nearly 24% of dialysis sessions are still missed, not because patients don’t care, but because of scheduling conflicts, lack of timely updates, or simple system inefficiencies. 

Furthermore, each missed session is not just a lost opportunity for care, but also an immediate revenue loss for hospitals, since empty slots cannot be filled last minute. By reducing missed appointments and improving attendance by 15–30%, NephroCare ensures patients receive consistent treatment while easing operational strain for medical institutions. Our system continuously tracks key health indicators, enabling smarter, more timely medical decisions and significantly reducing emergency visits and hospitalizations. At the same time, it streamlines scheduling and administrative workflows, allowing healthcare providers to focus on what actually matters, saving lives.

# Features

- Patient data preprocessing pipeline
- Feature engineering for clinical biomarkers
- Machine learning-based risk prediction
- Model validation with cross-validation techniques
- Modular architecture for healthcare analytics
- Daily Goals and Heath Tips
